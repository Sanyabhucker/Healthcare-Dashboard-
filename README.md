Healthcare Waiting Time Dashboard
Excited to share my PBI project.
Overview

I built this Power BI dashboard to explore healthcare waiting times from 2018 to 2021. The goal was to understand how long patients are waiting, how it changes over time, and which areas are most affected.

What I looked at
Average vs median waiting times
Distribution of patients across time bands
Differences between Outpatient, Day Case, and Inpatient
Trends over time
Key findings
A large number of patients fall into longer waiting time bands (especially 18+ months)
Outpatient cases make up most of the total volume
The gap between average and median shows the data is skewed, with some very long waits
